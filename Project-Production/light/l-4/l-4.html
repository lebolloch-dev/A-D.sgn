PYRA

Pyra is a floor lamp. It was thought to create a flow direction in a large space, public or private.

Pyra is made from a perforated, cut and bent steel sheet. The bulb passes through a welded guide inside the pyramid.

Its pyramidal shape, the base of which is openwork, generates two different light sources: a direct one, flush with the ground, on the three sides of the base; and an indirect second, via the perforated faces, which projects patterns throughout the room.
