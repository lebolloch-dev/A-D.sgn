OSTRA

The objective of this competition was to promote an unpopular material : expanded polystyrene, by designing an innovative object

Ostra is a tray with two containers, to offer a large quantity of seafood to customers, and a central column fastening the two trays, serving to keep a bottle of wine cool thanks to the isothermal character of the PSE.

This object aims to bring more people around the table and have a good time. Its fresh bottle and its original presentation make this tray a must for seafood tasting.



Client: EcoPSE (2nd price in innovative product category) Date: April 2014
