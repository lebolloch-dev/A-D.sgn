LABO T.

Labo T. is an electronic music label founded in 2016 by Teho . This sound laboratory is a place where artists can express their emotions. A cross between acoustic and electronic, hybrid styles, eclectic genres.

With Teho , I conceived a minimalist and monochromatic visual identity for this particular label. This design reflects both the completeness and the depth of the music released under the Labo T. label.
